# Phishing Email Investigation & Threat Analysis

## Objective
To analyse a suspicious email and identify indicators of phishing and potential security threats.

---

## Scenario
A user received an email claiming to be from a trusted organisation requesting urgent account verification. The objective was to analyse the email and determine whether it was legitimate or malicious.

---

## Analysis Process
- Examined sender email address for authenticity  
- Checked embedded links for mismatched or suspicious URLs  
- Analysed email content for urgency, tone, and manipulation tactics  
- Reviewed formatting, grammar, and inconsistencies  

---

## Indicators of Compromise (IOCs)
- Suspicious sender domain not matching official source  
- Embedded link redirecting to a non-legitimate website  
- Urgent language pressuring immediate action  
- Poor grammar and inconsistent formatting  

---

## Findings
- Email identified as a phishing attempt  
- Designed to trick users into revealing sensitive information  
- High risk of credential theft  

---

## Risk Impact
- Potential compromise of user credentials  
- Unauthorized system access  
- Data breach risk  

---

## Recommendations
- Do not click on suspicious links or download attachments  
- Verify sender identity through official channels  
- Report phishing attempts to IT/security team  
- Implement email filtering and user awareness training  

---

## Skills Demonstrated
- Threat identification  
- Phishing analysis  
- Risk assessment  
- Security awareness
